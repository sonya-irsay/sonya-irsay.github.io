Toss

iOS app, javascript.

Minimal iOS recreation of the 1970-90 toy Waterful Ring Toss. The goal is to use your touch to launch the hoops in the water and loop them onto the pegs. It has no score, no levels, no built-in user gratification – the player is invited to return to the basics of the childhood handheld game.

The app features water physics simulation, and renews itself after completion, with randomized peg layouts.
