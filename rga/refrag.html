Refrag Festival

Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop

Marketing materials produced for Refrag Festival 2018 - an annual symposium exploring new connections between art, culture and technology in Paris, France.

Videos and animated gifs were used to promote the event on social media. All content responds to the theme and structure of this edition of Refrag: Life Cycle Assessment, a technique used to assess all stages of a product’s life, from cradle to grave. Artists, speakers, performers and students were invited to engage with various aspects of this linear system: Plan – Extract – Transform – Produce – Consume – Use – Bin.

Assets were delivered as short and long videos, animated gifs and stills.
