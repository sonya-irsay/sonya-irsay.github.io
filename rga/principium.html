Sanie Irsay CV

:Principium

Risograph print, 2 colors, on Munken Print 300g paper, printed and distributed in limited editions. Client: Le Hub Lafayette Anticipation Gallery.

Visual identity for the MFA Design and Technology Parsons Paris graduate show. Each exhibiting artist was asked to create a shape from an A4 sheet of paper, which represented them throughout the show in posters, postcards, business cards, exhibition catalogues and wall text.
