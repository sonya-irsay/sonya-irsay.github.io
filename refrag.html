Refrag Website

HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

Website re-design and development for the fourth edition of Refrag, an annual symposium exploring new connections between art, culture and technology in Paris, France. This one-page website celebrates early internet and glitch aesthetics, using simple typographic animations and bold colors.
