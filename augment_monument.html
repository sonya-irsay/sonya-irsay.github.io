Augment Monument

AR App. Unity, C#, ARKit

The public monument is an authoritarian device used to elevate, singularize and thus show official state narrative. The statues placed on the pedestal occupy a place of importance, power, “heroism,” whiteness and masculinity. Augment Monument is conceived as a tool to anonymize something that is meant to embody the opposite – it allows the user to filter out the heroes we are provided by the state, and to reclaim, even if passively, the substance of our public space.
