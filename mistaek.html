Mistaek Magazine

Issue I, perfect bound, 160 pages.

Mistaek Magazine is about mistakes. Mistaek Magazine makes mistakes. The magazine’s conception coincided with, or rather came out of, the grave and radical changes rapidly unfolding on a global scale in the period around November 2016.

Mistaek Magazine tries to oppose the tendency to regress into cynicism or nihilism, instead proposing the possibility of cultivating self-conscious, critical reflection. Mistaek Mag seeks to examine humankind’s immense database of everything that has gone wrong, from silly typos to severe global disasters to the faults inherent in the design field itself, aiming to look critically at itself, at ourselves.
