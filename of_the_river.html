Of the river, of the forest

Silkscreen, 33 x 45 cm, edition of 20, 7 colors, on white, gray and cream Arches paper.

Silkscreen poster reflecting on my experience in North Borneo, Malaysia. I was told by a local resident that orang utang means “people of the forest” in Malay, while the people that belong to the area’s indigenous tribe are called orang sungai – “people of the river.”
