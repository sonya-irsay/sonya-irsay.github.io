Presentation Templates for Parsons Paris, The New School

Adobe InDesign. PowerPoint, Google Slides

As Program Assistant at Parsons Paris, I designed slideshow presentation templates to be used across all departments. At the time of my arrival, Parsons Paris communication lacked coherency and adherence to current branding. I worked in collaboration with faculty, communication managers, administration and the student body to condense their different goals into a unified image of the school.

The templates were delivered in Adobe InDesign (for experienced designers), Powerpoint (for administration and faculty) and Google Slides (for easy sharing and collaborating). The templates strictly adhere to all New School branding rules, including colors, fonts and assets, while staying unique to the Paris university campus.
