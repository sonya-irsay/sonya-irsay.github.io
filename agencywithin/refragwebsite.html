Refrag Website

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Website re-design and development for the fourth edition of Refrag, an annual symposium exploring new connections between art, culture and technology in Paris, France.

This simple one-page website provides all the information about the festival including date and time, guest speakers, schedule and description. It celebrates early internet and glitch aesthetics, using simple typographic animations and bold colors.
