De Natura Rerum

80 pages, Risograph print (3 colors) on white and fluo green stock, edition of 200.

Collaborative edition of Lucretius’ De Natura Rerum produced as part of You Are Here exhibition during the COP21 conference. The text was illustrated using only scanned images from found secondhand books.

Collaborative project led by Aurélien Farina.
